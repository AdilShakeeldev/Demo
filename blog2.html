3 Easy Steps to Fight Common Skin Concerns.

Conquer common skin concerns with 3 easy steps using natural ingredients!

3 Easy steps for removing tan from your face:

Step 1: Exfoliate with gram flour (besan): Make a paste by mixing gram flour with yogurt or milk. Apply it gently to your face and massage in circular motions for a minute. This removes dead skin cells and helps reduce tan. Wash it off with cool water.

Step 2: Brighten with lemon and honey: This is a classic combination for a reason! Mix one tablespoon of lemon juice with one tablespoon of honey. Apply this paste to your face and leave it on for 10 minutes (be careful not to leave it on for too long, especially if you have sensitive skin). Lemon juice helps lighten the tan, while honey soothes and moisturizes your skin. Wash it off with cool water.

Step 3: Hydrate with yogurt: Plain yogurt is a natural coolant and moisturizer. Apply a thick layer of yogurt to your face and leave it on for 20 minutes. Rinse with cool water. Yogurt will help soothe any irritation caused by the sun and keep your skin hydrated.

Remember: Be consistent and do a patch test before applying to your face. These are natural remedies and may take time to show results.

3 easy steps for removing acne scars from your face

Step 1:Exfoliate & Renew: Make a paste with 1 tbsp gram flour (besan) and a pinch of turmeric powder. Apply gently and massage in circular motions for 1 minute. Rinse with cool water. Gram flour exfoliates, while turmeric helps reduce inflammation and promotes healing.

Step 2:Brighten & Soothe: Mix 1 tsp of aloe vera gel with a few drops of honey. Apply the paste for 15 minutes and rinse with cool water. Aloe vera soothes and promotes healing, while honey brightens the scars.

Step 3:Moisturize & Protect: Apply a thin layer of cold-pressed coconut oil to hydrate and protect the skin.

Remember: Be consistent and do a patch test before applying to your face. These are natural remedies and may take time to show results.

3 Steps to Instant Glowing Skin

Step 1: Exfoliate & Brighten - Mix 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan) with 1 tablespoon milk or rose water,(can also add half a teaspoon of coffee). Massage gently for 1 minute. Rinse with cool water. (Gram flour buffs away dullness, milk/rose water brightens)

Step 2: Hydrate & Plump- Mash 1 tomato (remember to remove the seeds) Apply the paste for 15 minutes. Rinse with cool water.(Tomatoes are rich in vitamin C, tomatoes are antioxidants that can help fight free radicals which damage skin cells and contribute to dullness )

Step 3:Glow Boost - Apply a thin layer of chilled aloe vera gel. Leave on for 10 minutes or use as a primer. Rinse with cool water (optional). (Aloe vera hydrates and soothes, giving a natural glow)

Bonus Tip: Splash your face with cool rose water after cleansing for an extra refreshing feel.

Remember: Be consistent and do a patch test before applying to your face. These are natural remedies and may take time to show results.

Reduce pigmentation from your face using Indian ingredients, in 3 easy steps:

Step 1:Exfoliate & Brighten; Mix 1 tbsp gram flour (besan) with 1 tsp yogurt. Massage gently for 1 minute. Rinse with cool water. (Gram flour buffs away dullness, yogurt brightens with lactic acid)

Step 2:Lighten & Soothe; Mash 1/2 papaya (rich in enzymes) with 1 tsp honey. Apply the paste for 15 minutes. Rinse with cool water. (Papaya helps lighten, honey soothes and hydrates)

Step 3:Brighten & Protect; Apply a thin layer of turmeric paste (mix with milk or water). Leave on for 10 minutes. (Turmeric brightens and reduces inflammation) Rinse with cool water and follow with sunscreen! (Sunscreen prevents further pigmentation)
